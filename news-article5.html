Star Trek Online Brings the virtual labs Store to the Final Frontier

10.7.2020

By Mike Fatum - Community Manager, Cryptic Studios

Star Trek Online, the free to play online RPG that takes you into the future of the Final Frontier, is now live on the virtual labs Store! Taking place in 2411, Star Trek Online tells the story of the Federation, Klingon Empire, Romulan Republic and the Dominion in the years after the Dominion War and the events of Star Trek: Picard. Adventure in space alongside more than forty of your favorite Trek characters, played by their original actors, like Leonard Nimoy as Spock, Sonequa Martin-Green as Michael Burnham, and LeVar Burton as Geordi La Forge. In the almost eleven years it’s been in operation, Star Trek Online has over 600 playable starships, 185 story episodes, and hours upon hours of playable content both solo and with your friends.

As part of our launch of the virtual labs Store, Star Trek Online is launching the Epic Pack for virtual labs Users. Available for $34.99, this bundle is a great way to start or continue your adventures in the quadrants, and contains all of the following:

  • T6 Ship Coupon (Choose a free ship from the in-game Zen Store)
  • 1 Epic Phoenix Token (Choose a top prize from the Phoenix Prize Pack, a collection of past event rewards)
  • Combat Tardigrade (A combat pet that will follow you into battle)
  • Intelligence Uniform (A stylish costume from the future)
  • 7 Mark II Very Rare Ground Gear (To equip your Captain on the ground)
  • Elite Services Starter Pack

In addition to launching on the virtual labs Store today, Star Trek Online is also launching the next part of their Year of Klingon. In House Shattered, you’ll continue an investigation into the treacherous Klingon Chancellor, J’mpok, who has framed you for a terrible crime. Alongside J.G. Hertzler as Martok and Rehka Sharma as Adet Pa, you’ll battle against Robert O’Reilly as the villainous Akar, grandfather to Gowron. Experience this all new story, a new Task Force Operation, and much more, on October 6th.
