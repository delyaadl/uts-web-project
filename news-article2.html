Bugsnax available November 12! Get 15% off your pre-order. Learn about the game’s cast and gameplay right here.

10.13.2020

By Kevin Zuhn - Creative Director

Hey snak fans, the news is out: Bugsnax will be releasing on November 12th! You can also get 15% off if you pre-order now, so no time to waste.

Bugsnax takes you on a whimsical adventure to Snaktooth Island, home of the legendary half-bug half-snack creatures, Bugsnax. Invited by intrepid explorer Elizabert Megafig, you arrive to discover your host nowhere to be found, her camp in shambles, and her followers scattered across the island alone... and hungry!

It's up to you to solve the mysteries of Snaktooth Island: What happened to Lizbert? What are Bugsnax and where do they come from? But most of all, why do they taste SO GOOD?

Today, we’ve also revealed our amazing cast of voice actors! Here’s a couple that might sound familiar: Max Mittelman (Saitama in One-Punch Man), Fryda Wolff (Loba in Apex Legends), Haviland Stillwell (Devil May Cry 5), Debra Wilson (Jedi: Fallen Order), Roger Craig Smith (Sonic the Hedgehog), Yuri Lowenthal (Spider-Man PS4), and Fred Tatasciore (Overwatch). What a list! We’re grateful to have worked with such an all-star lineup, and we can’t wait for you to hear how they’ve brought our game to life. If you’re curious, you can find the full list on our Twitter account right now (@YoungHorses)!

And we’re not done yet! We’ve given some news sites and influencers hands-on previews with Bugsnax, and their first impressions are now live! Head on over to your favorite games news site to find out who’s talkin’ bout Bugsnax.

On that note, thanks for checking in with us and we’ll see you on November 12th!
