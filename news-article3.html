Seize Victory in the Marvel Knockout Super Series in Fortnite and Suit Up as Daredevil

10.12.2020

By The Fortnite Team

Fortnite fans,
You may have teamed up with many Legends of Marvel so far in the Fortnite Nexus War. However, another has emerged from the Rift between worlds, arriving not only to defeat Galactus but to initiate a test of courage on the Island.

Get ready to assemble for Chapter 2 - Season 4’s Marvel Knockout Super Series, four competitive cups featuring the Marvel Knockout Limited Time Mode! The first of these cups — happening this Wednesday, October 14 — celebrates the Man Without Fear himself, Daredevil. For finishing as one of the top teams in a region, winners of this Trios cup will get early access to the Daredevil Outfit before its release in the Item Shop!

The next three cups in the Marvel Knockout Super Series will take place over the course of November, with details for each arriving a couple days before. Any player who participates in all four cups will receive the special Nexus War Glider! And to conclude the series in a super way, all four cups will build up to a final, $1,000,000 tournament happening on November 21.

If you need a TL;DR for Marvel Knockout, this mode is a spin on Chapter 2 - Season 2’s Operation: Knockout where your team employs super-powers to be the best team on the four-round bracket. You and your teammates will be granted a different set of powers each round for you to battle rivals with mirrored abilities!

For the official Daredevil Cup rules, you can find them at the Daredevil Cup rules page on Fortnite.com. And for the details on the following cups, make sure to stay tuned to Fortnite.com and our social channels.
